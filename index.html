Fostering a kitten is the best

When shelters get overcrowded, fostering cats or kittens is a great opportunity to get more involved. This frees up space and resources for shelters, allowing them to rescue and assist even more animals, which is particularly important during kitten season. It also provides a friendly environment for cats and kittens that have not been adopted to reside in until they can find a forever home

For more information, visit RSPCA website.
